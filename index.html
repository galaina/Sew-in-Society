Sew-in Society

Bringing your inner Fashion Designer to Life

Who We Are

A social community for anyone wanting to learn the skills of sewing for all purposes, whether it be as a hobby

We provide a fully equipped studio space for creatives to create and work on their small fashion projects.

We also hold workshops for basic sewing skills and pattern cutting from our unique Team of skilled and industry experienced Members

Meet our Team

Susanna - Our Founder and Head Design Teacher

" Here at Social Sewing we want to create an interative space where our members can. learn and create in a lively environment. I have met many individuals who would love to learnt he bas ic skills of sewing as a life social skill but dont know where to turn for a personable experience; so it led me to form this studio space for all who desire to learn. practice and create can do so, with others members close by and have the support they require."

Christine - Design Assistant and Freelance Pattern Cutter

"I love knowing that we have a space in town for creatives of all levels to come togtehr. not only is this studio spacious, and the perfect environment to create but it is also a hub of community, a platform to network and build genuine connections. I came across the studio when I was searching for a space to pattern cut away from small single room and three years later i am so glad i dropped Susan that private message on Facebook!. I can not only work freely, but i get to help others to learn this great skill."

Carlos - In-House Machine Technician and Freelance Designer

"Social Sewing became my Second Home three years ago, straight out of grad school- UAL ; and i have loved everyday since. not only do I get to share my knowledge and love for fashion design but i get to see others fall in love with it too. People dont just come to learn, but they come to find a second home, just like i did. i am so glad we ar Sewing School and creating spaces for creativity and long lasting connection"
