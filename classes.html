Sewing Society Classes and Workshops

Experience the joy of sewing in a fun and supportive environment. Join our sewing society and meet cool people while learning new skills. Our community is all about creativity, collaboration, and enjoying the art of sewing together.

Classes

Monday
12pm-4pm

Pattern Cutting

This class is suitable for both beginners and intermediate level participants. Learn the art of pattern cutting and enhance your sewing skills.

Tuesday
12pm-4pm

Basic Sewing

Perfect for beginners, this class focuses on fundamental sewing techniques. Get hands-on experience and build a strong foundation in sewing.

Wednesday
10am-6pm

All Day Open Workspace

Join us for an entire day of open workspace. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, this is a welcoming space for all sewing enthusiasts.

Thursday
12pm-4pm

Basic Sewing

Similar to Tuesday's class, Thursday's session caters to both beginners and intermediate sewers. Refine your sewing skills in a supportive environment.

Friday
10am-4pm

All Day Open Workspace - with Happy Social Evening

Enjoy an extended open workspace on Fridays, and join us for a happy social evening. Connect with fellow sewing enthusiasts in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere.

Sunday
11am-3pm

All Day Open Workspace

Sundays are for open workspace all day. Whether you want to start a new project or need assistance with an ongoing one, this is the perfect time to sew and socialize.
